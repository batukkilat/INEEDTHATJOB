How to set up email sending

Gmail (recommended)

  1. Go to myaccount.google.com → Security and enable 2-Step Verification.
  2. Under Security, find App passwords.
  3. Select app: Mail, device: Other → type "INEEDTHATJOB" → click Generate.
  4. Copy the 16-character password. Your regular password won't work.
  5. Use these values:
    Host: smtp.gmail.com Port: 587
    Username & From Email: your Gmail address
    Password: the 16-character app password

Outlook / Hotmail

  1. Host: smtp.office365.com Port: 587
  2. Username & Password: your Outlook credentials.

Other providers

Search "your provider SMTP settings" — every major email service has a help page.

Credentials are saved only to .env on this computer. Never sent elsewhere.
